Diet

A pandas diet consists of bamboo roots and other vegetarian options.

They eat for 10-16 hours a day.

Pandas eat food quickly using their strong teeth.

Pandas have the same digestive system as a carnivore,

they lack the enzymes needed to break down other plants.

Habitat

Giant Panda

Giant pandas live in southwest of China,

more specifically in the Yangtze Basin region.

They prefer cool and wet bamboo forests.

Red pandas

Red pandas are omnivores.

They eay bamboo, fruit and small insects.

They live in the same area as giant pandas

Climbing

Giant pandas can climb up to 13,000 feet to feed on high slopes.

Pandas learn to climb at age five months old.

Paws

Pandas have six digits on their front paws.

They use indistinguishable like pseudo-thumbs to grip bamboo stems and hold food.
